TV Tracker Website

This website tracks and recommends tv shows and programs for a user. It allows you to create an account and add shows to your profile through the use of icons. This was a capstone project I did with a couple of my classmates, and it taught me a lot about web development and deployment! I did all of the fronend work and a little bit of python in the backend to make our icon buttons work. (Django)

Spotify Website

This website allows a user to log into their spotify account and see their top content on their profile. The content can be seen through 3 different time ranges (short term, medium term, and long term). Tracks are recommended to users, and users can also discover new music by looking through different artists. Throughout this project I got pretty comfortable with APIs and React became my favorite framework. (React)
